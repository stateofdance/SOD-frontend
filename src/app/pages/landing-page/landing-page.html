Our Story

A premier dance institution founded on passion, artistry, and excellence. Established by renowned dancer and choreographer Kervin Mendiola, the studio began as a vision to create a space where dancers of all ages could express themselves, grow with confidence, and find purpose through movement.

From its humble beginnings to its elegant studios in Quezon City, BGC, and Manila, State of Dance has become a home for aspiring and professional dancers alike. We offer dance classes for kids, teens, and adults, private training, recital programs, studio rentals, and professional performance services for corporate and special events.

Each class and performance is crafted to go beyond technique — cultivating artistry, discipline, and self-expression. At State of Dance, we don’t just teach choreography; we shape character, build confidence, and celebrate the elegance of transformation.
